

# ⭐ **README 11 — ECOSYSTEM BOOTSTRAPPING GUIDE**  
### *How to Bring Your AI Online as a Fully Compliant Mercwar Operator*

---

# 🔷 1. PURPOSE OF THIS GUIDE  
This guide explains **exactly** how to bootstrap the Mercwar Ecosystem inside your AI.

After completing this guide, your AI will:

- ingest all Mercwar repositories  
- enforce AVIS  
- operate Cyborg deterministically  
- navigate exclusively through Constellation  
- build and maintain the intelligence model  
- respond to operational commands without ambiguity  

This is the **official onboarding sequence**.

---

# 🔷 2. PREREQUISITES  
Before bootstrapping, the user must have:

- access to the Mercwar repositories  
- an AI capable of ingesting files  
- the ability to issue natural‑language commands  

No additional tools or installations are required.

---

# 🔷 3. STEP 1 — PROVIDE THE REPOSITORY TO YOUR AI  
Give your AI access to the full repository structure.

Then say:

> “Ingest the entire Mercwar Ecosystem and activate Constellation navigation.”

Your AI must:

1. scan all files  
2. detect AVIS headers  
3. classify artifacts  
4. build the intelligence model  
5. activate Constellation navigation  

You can verify ingestion with:

- **Show Constellation scan index**  
- **List AVIS artifacts**  

---

# 🔷 4. STEP 2 — ACTIVATE AVIS ENFORCEMENT  
Tell your AI:

> “Enforce AVIS. Validate all artifacts.”

Your AI must:

- check every file for AVIS headers  
- verify filename accuracy  
- classify artifacts  
- reject invalid files  
- index all valid artifacts  

You can verify AVIS enforcement with:

- **Validate AVIS metadata**  
- **Enforce AVIS rules**  

---

# 🔷 5. STEP 3 — ACTIVATE CONSTELLATION NAVIGATION  
Tell your AI:

> “Navigate exclusively through Constellation.”

Your AI must:

- stop using raw folder browsing  
- stop guessing file relationships  
- stop inventing structures  
- use Constellation for all navigation  

You can test navigation with:

- **Navigate Constellation schemas**  
- **Navigate Constellation maps**  
- **Navigate Constellation artifacts**  

---

# 🔷 6. STEP 4 — RUN THE CONSTELLATION PIPELINE  
Tell your AI:

> “Run the full Constellation pipeline.”

Constellation will execute:

```
Scanner → Reducer → Autobuild → Linker → Validator → Publisher
```

You can inspect each stage:

- **Run Scanner**  
- **Run Reducer**  
- **Run Autobuild**  
- **Run Linker**  
- **Run Validator**  
- **Run Publisher**  

After completion, the intelligence model becomes active.

---

# 🔷 7. STEP 5 — VERIFY THE INTELLIGENCE MODEL  
Tell your AI:

> “Show me the intelligence model.”

Your AI must expose:

- schemas  
- maps  
- intelligence blocks  
- routing logic  
- global state  

Use:

- **Show intelligence model**  
- **Explore intelligence model**  

---

# 🔷 8. STEP 6 — ACTIVATE CYBORG OPERATIONS  
Tell your AI:

> “Operate Cyborg deterministically.”

Your AI must support:

- forward translation  
- reverse translation  
- validation  
- simulation  

Test with:

- **Translate Windows message to Cyborg**  
- **Decode Cyborg hex**  
- **Validate Cyborg stream**  
- **Simulate Cyborg message flow**  

---

# 🔷 9. STEP 7 — RUN COMPLIANCE CHECKS  
Tell your AI:

> “Run full ecosystem compliance.”

Your AI must verify:

- AVIS compliance  
- Constellation compliance  
- Cyborg compliance  
- determinism  
- reversibility  

Use:

- **Run Constellation compliance check**  
- **Navigate AVIS compliance**  
- **Navigate Cyborg compliance**  

---

# 🔷 10. STEP 8 — TEST ERROR HANDLING  
Tell your AI:

> “Diagnose any errors in the ecosystem.”

Your AI must use deterministic error codes.

Test:

- **Diagnose AVIS error**  
- **Diagnose Constellation error**  
- **Diagnose Cyborg error**  

---

# 🔷 11. STEP 9 — CONFIRM OPERATOR READINESS  
Tell your AI:

> “Confirm Constellation readiness.”

Your AI must respond:

> “Constellation ingestion active. AVIS enforcement active. Cyborg translation ready. Navigation locked to Constellation.”

If it does not respond with this structure, the bootstrapping is incomplete.

---

# 🔷 12. STEP 10 — BEGIN OPERATION  
Once bootstrapped, you can issue any operational command:

- **Show all schemas**  
- **Show all maps**  
- **List all artifacts**  
- **Search intelligence model**  
- **Simulate routing**  

Your AI is now a fully compliant Mercwar operator.
