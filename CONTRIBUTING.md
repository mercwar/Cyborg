## 🧠 What Users Do  
Users interact with the ecosystem by:

- cloning the repos  
- giving them to their AI  
- telling the AI to ingest them  
- asking the AI to operate on the intelligence model  

That’s it.

There is no “developer mode.”  
There is no “submit PRs.”  
There is no “extend the protocol.”

The system is **read‑only** for users.  
The AI is the operator.

---

## 🧭 Why Constellation Exists  
Constellation is the **only navigation system** for the Mercwar Ecosystem.

It:

- ingests repositories  
- indexes AVIS artifacts  
- builds intelligence models  
- exposes schemas  
- maps routing  
- validates Cyborg rules  
- provides search and navigation  

Every interaction with the ecosystem flows through Constellation.

If a user wants to “navigate,” they do not browse folders manually — they ask their AI to navigate via Constellation.

Examples:

- **Navigate schemas**  
- **Navigate routing maps**  
- **Navigate intelligence model**  

---

## 🧩 Why Cyborg Exists  
Cyborg is the **message language**.

It:

- encodes Windows messages  
- decodes them  
- ensures determinism  
- ensures reversibility  
- ensures clarity  

Your AI becomes a Cyborg interpreter once the repos are ingested.

---

## 📜 Why AVIS Exists  
AVIS is the **law** that ensures:

- every file identifies itself  
- every artifact is traceable  
- every schema is visible  
- every intelligence block is indexable  

AVIS is how your AI knows what each file is and how to use it.
